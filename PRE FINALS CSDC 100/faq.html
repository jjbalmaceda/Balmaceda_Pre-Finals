What is Generative AI?

Generative AI refers to AI algorithms capable of producing realistic, seemingly original content. Examples include essays, short-form content, books, lifelike images, art, and audio clips.

How does it work?

Generative AI uses neural networks and large datasets to generate new content that mimics the patterns and styles of existing data.

What can GenAI do?

GenAI can create articles, generate images, design music, assist with coding, and help brainstorm ideas.

What are its limitations or risks?

Limitations include inaccurate outputs, bias, over-reliance on AI, and ethical concerns about misuse of generated content.
