Ethical Use of Generative AI

Understand the key ethical considerations when using Generative AI responsibly.

Bias and Fairness

Generative AI can inherit biases from its training data. Recognize and mitigate bias to ensure fairness in outputs.

Privacy Protection

AI should not generate content that violates personal privacy. Always respect user data and consent when applying AI tools.

Transparency

Be clear when content is AI-generated. Transparency builds trust and prevents misinformation about the source of content.

Responsible Use

Use Generative AI ethically to avoid harmful content, plagiarism, or misuse. Ensure outputs align with societal and professional norms.

Intellectual Property

Respect copyright and intellectual property laws. Do not claim AI-generated content as your own or infringe on existing works.

Accountability

Always take responsibility for the AI outputs you use. Monitor, evaluate, and correct content to ensure ethical standards are met.
